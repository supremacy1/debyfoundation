Deby's Foundation Scholarship

The Debys Foundation Scholarship is designed to support undergraduates in achieving their educational goals. Below are the eligibility criteria:

  • Must be currently enrolled as an undergraduate student in a recognized institution.
  • Must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 (on a 4.0 or a 5.0 scale) or equivalent.
  • Demonstrate financial need through appropriate documentation.
  • Open to students from all disciplines of study.
  • Applicants must not have received another major scholarship in the same academic year.
  • Must be under 30 years of age at the time of application.
  • Must provide a prove of identification as a citizen of Oboburu community
  • Must not exhibit any anti-social character
