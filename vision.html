Welcome to the Deby Foundation Scholarship

Our Vision

The Deby Foundation Scholarship envisions a world where every undergraduate student, regardless of their background or field of study, has the opportunity to succeed academically and professionally. We are committed to providing the resources, mentorship, and support to help students achieve their dreams and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Key Vision Goals

  • Empowering Future Innovators: Encouraging students to develop innovative solutions for global challenges.
  • Fostering Inclusivity: Ensuring that students from all backgrounds feel valued and supported.
  • Building Lifelong Skills: Providing students with the tools and experiences necessary to succeed long after graduation.
  • Connecting Global Networks: Creating a worldwide community of scholars who support each other's growth and opportunities.
  • Inspiring Leadership: Helping students develop the leadership skills to drive positive change in their communities and beyond.
  • Promoting Sustainable Development: Encouraging students to pursue careers that contribute to the well-being of the planet and society.
