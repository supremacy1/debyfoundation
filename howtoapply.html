How to Apply

Follow these simple steps to apply for the Derby's Foundation Scholarship:

  1. Step 1: Check Eligibility
    Ensure you meet the basic eligibility criteria such as academic standing, financial need, and specific field of study.
  2. Step 2: Prepare Your Documents
    Gather the required documents including your letter of admission, academic credentials, letters of recommendation from your family secretary, L.G.A ID, recent passport photograph, NIN and personal statement.
  3. Step 3: Fill Out the Application Form
    Complete the online application form available on our website. Be sure to provide all necessary information.
  4. Step 4: Submit Your Application
    Review your application, and submit it before the deadline. Make sure all your documents are uploaded correctly.
  5. Step 5: Wait for Results
    After submitting your application, wait for the announcement of the successful candidates. You will be notified via email.
